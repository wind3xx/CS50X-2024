sort1 uses: Bubble sort

How do you know?: It's the fastest in sorted text files. O(n) <- Formula only present in sorted files, otherwise O(n^2).

sort2 uses: Merge sort

How do you know?: It's the fastest in reversed text files and random text files. O(n*log(n))

sort3 uses: Selection sort

How do you know?: It's the slowest in sorted text files. O(n^2)
